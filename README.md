# Mendel

- **Website:** mendel.com
- **Category:** Enterprise spend management platform (LatAm)
- **Core markets:** Mexico (90% growth focus), Argentina, Chile
- **Customer base:** 1,000+ large enterprises (KPMG, OXXO/FEMSA, Walmart, Suzuki, Viva Aerobus)
- **Status:** Onboarding complete (Apr 28, 2026), GTM playbook generated May 4, 2026

## Product Modules
1. Corporate Cards + Smart Spend Controls (real-time policy engine)
2. Expense Management (replaces SAP Concur + Excel + AMEX)
3. Mendel Viajes (Sabre + Amadeus certified OBT, BCD/Altour/Cocha/TS/Furlong partnered)
4. ERP Integrations (SAP S/4HANA, Oracle Cloud, Netsuite)
5. Invoice Retrieval / CFDI Recovery (Mexico-only, +30% deductibility)
6. AI Layer (anomaly detection, WhatsApp receipt capture, AI travel booking)

## Commercial Profile
- **ACV:** USD $15K – $20K / year ($700 – $1,800 / month)
- **Sales cycle:** 3 – 6 months (some enterprise 12+ months)
- **LTV:** Effectively infinite – near-zero churn
- **Conversion:** Inbound 18%, Outbound 12%, SDR 7%, WhatsApp 8%, Global 17%
- **CRM:** HubSpot
- **Q2-2026 goal:** 100+ qualified sales meetings per quarter
